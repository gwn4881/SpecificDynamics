DEMETER Platform

The DEMETER Platform is a versatile agricultural drone designed to help farmers improve crop yields and optimize their farming operations. Equipped with advanced sensors and imaging technology, the drone can provide real-time information on crop and soil metrics, as well as production animal metrics. The drone's advanced sensors can measure soil moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels, providing farmers with valuable insights into the health of their crops and soil. The drone can also capture high-resolution images of crops, allowing farmers to monitor plant health, growth, and yield. The DEMETER Platform is also equipped with animal monitoring capabilities, allowing farmers to keep track of livestock health and performance. The drone can capture data on animal movement, behavior, and feed consumption, helping farmers optimize their livestock production and improve animal welfare.

Abilities and Features

  • High-resolution imaging capabilities for monitoring plant health, growth, and yield And Animal Health and welfare
  • Advanced technology for providing valuable insights into crop and soil health
  • Real-time remote monitoring capabilities for improved efficiency
  • Advanced Heat detection for production animal AI
