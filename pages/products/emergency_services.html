CHIRON Platform

The CHIRON Platform is a highly capable emergency services drone designed for rapid deployment in critical situations. Equipped with state-of-the-art sensors and imaging technology, this drone is an essential tool for first responders and emergency personnel. Equipped with life saving devices such as a standalone AED (defibrillator) unit and a small reserve of oxygen to help trapped indivduals hold out for rescue. The CHIRON also has a range of communication capabilities, including voice and video streaming, allowing remote communication with responders on the ground. Additionally, the drone has a built-in global positioning system (GPS) and a range of advanced flight modes, enabling it to navigate challenging environments and execute complex flight patterns.

Abilities and Features

  • High End Military Grade Imaging Hardware
  • Equipped with Emergency Medical tools
  • Capable of allowing commuication between trapped or stuck victims and First Responders
  • "Clearing Mode" - The CHIRON will check rooms in a building, finding and reporting indivduals in need of help, while also identifing threats from fires, shooters, bombs, violent animals, etc...
