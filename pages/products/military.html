ATLAS Platform

The Specific Dynamics ATLAS Platform is a military-grade, modular drone. Capable of supporting troops on the battlefield in a large number of scenarios. From Fire Support to Electronic Warfare, the applications of this drone are near endless.

Abilities and Features

  • Capable of Autonomous Scouting Operations
  • Advanced Imaging Array
  • High End Communication Array(LF, HF, UHF, SHF, Satellite)
  • 360 Degree Weapon Carrage capable of utilizing crew served weaponry such as the Mark 19 Automatic Grenade Launcher and the Browning M2 50 Cal Machine Gun
